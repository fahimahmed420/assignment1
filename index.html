Pillers of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

The journey towards becoming a middle-income country by 2030 demands bold and innovative solutions to overcome the nation's challenges.

Sustainbility

The Sustainable Development Goals are a global call to action to end poverty, protect the earth’s environment and climate, and ensure that people everywhere can enjoy peace and prosperity.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh's infrastructure includes energy, transportation, urban, education, and health systems.

Empowerment

Bangladesh has made significant progress in empowering women in many areas, including education, health, and political representation.

PThe Vision of Future

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation in Bangladesh is the process of integrating digital technology into all aspects of the country's operations.

Green Energy

As of June 2023, renewable energy constitutes 4.5 percent of the total installed power capacity in Bangladesh, with 1,183 MW out of 22,215 MW coming from renewable sources, predominantly solar power.

Modern Infrastructure

Bangladesh's modern infrastructure includes roads, bridges, railways, power plants, and digital centers.
