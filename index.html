Pillers of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

The journey towards becoming a middle-income country by 2030 demands bold and innovative solutions to overcome the nation's challenges.

Sustainbility

To end poverty, protect the earth's environment and climate, and ensure that people everywhere can enjoy peace and prosperity.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure bottlenecks are a major obstacle to Bangladesh's economic growth. The country needs to invest in infrastructure to meet the needs of its growing population.

Empowerment

Bangladesh has made significant progress in empowering women in many areas, including education, health, and political representation.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation in Bangladesh is the process of integrating digital technology into all aspects of the country's operations.

Green Energy

4.5 percent of the total installed power capacity in Bangladesh, with 1,183 MW out of 22,215 MW coming from renewable sources

Modern Infrastructure

Bangladesh's modern infrastructure includes roads, bridges, railways, power plants, and digital centers.
